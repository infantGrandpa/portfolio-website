Pipette Claims App

While at Thermo Fisher Scientific, I needed to reconcile roughly 1200 pipettes with their proper owner, which could be any of about 400 different researchers. To circumvent the inevitable back-and-forth to do so, I designed and developed an app that simply allowed users to search for their pipette or location and claim them as their own.

AI Tag

A brief project designed to teach me how to develop AI using Behavior Trees. This consists of creating a "game" that requires no input from a player, but will play itself. Each AI person will play tag, chasing each other while avoiding obstacles and predicting others' movements.
