About

My Story

While going to school to earn a BS in Communications Media (read: Film), I had realized that it wasn’t the path for me but, with no better ideas of what I wanted, I set out to finish college and get the degree. My final semester, I took a course in programming in Python and it put me on a new trajectory.

After graduation, I began working on various projects from video games to databases to Excel macros. In that time, I’ve learned Visual Basic, C#, GML, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I found UX Design after realizing how much work I was willing to put in to make things easier for my users, but never had any formal training. I knew I enjoyed solving problems with others in mind but wasn’t always aware of the best ways to do so. With my UX Design certification at CareerFoundry, I’ve definitely developed a much better understanding of the process!

I also love video games and am always (at the very least) throwing around the idea of working on one.
